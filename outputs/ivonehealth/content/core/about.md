# About IV One Health

> **IV One Health** is a specialized infusion therapy center in Riyadh, Saudi Arabia, providing expert care for patients requiring complex infusion treatments.

---

## Our Mission

To provide exceptional infusion therapy services that support patients and physicians in managing complex medical conditions. We believe in:

- **Clinical Excellence** — Rigorous protocols and experienced staff
- **Patient Comfort** — A healing environment designed around patient needs
- **Physician Partnership** — Seamless coordination with referring providers
- **Continuous Improvement** — Staying current with advances in infusion therapy

---

## Our Clinical Team

IV One Health's clinical team includes:

- **Registered Nurses** — Specialized training in infusion therapy administration
- **Clinical Coordinators** — Managing patient scheduling and physician communication
- **Medical Director Oversight** — Ensuring clinical protocols meet highest standards

Our staff maintains current certifications and undergoes regular training on infusion protocols, medication handling, and emergency response.

---

## Our Facility

Our Riyadh infusion center features:

- **Private Infusion Suites** — Comfortable, individual treatment spaces
- **Medical-Grade Equipment** — Infusion pumps and monitoring technology
- **Emergency Preparedness** — Full resuscitation capabilities on-site
- **Comfortable Amenities** — WiFi, entertainment options, refreshments

## Accreditation & Compliance

IV One Health operates under the regulatory oversight of the Saudi Ministry of Health. Our facility maintains:

- Current MOH licensure
- Medication handling compliance
- Infection control protocols
- Patient safety standards

---

## Working With Physicians

We serve as an extension of your practice. When you refer a patient to IV One Health:

1. **Referral received** — We review the prescription and patient information
2. **Insurance coordination** — We verify coverage and handle authorizations
3. **Scheduling** — We contact the patient to arrange appointment times
4. **Treatment** — Infusion administered per your protocol
5. **Reporting** — You receive documentation after each treatment

[Contact us](/contact) to establish a referral relationship.
