## What is Iron Infusion Therapy?

Iron infusion therapy delivers iron directly into the bloodstream through an IV. This treatment is prescribed when oral iron supplements are insufficient, not tolerated, or when rapid iron replenishment is medically necessary.

## How Iron Infusion Works

Intravenous iron bypasses the digestive system to:

- Rapidly restore iron levels in the blood
- Replenish iron stores in the body
- Improve hemoglobin production
- Resolve symptoms of iron deficiency anemia

## When Iron Infusion is Recommended

Iron infusion may be recommended for patients who:

- Cannot tolerate oral iron supplements
- Have conditions limiting iron absorption
- Require rapid iron correction before procedures
- Have chronic conditions causing ongoing iron loss
