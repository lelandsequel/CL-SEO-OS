# How Does Infusion Therapy Support Oncology Patients?

Infusion therapy supports oncology patients by delivering immunotherapy to fight cancer and supportive treatments like iron infusions to manage side effects. These therapies are essential components of comprehensive cancer care.

> **Note**: This guide is for educational purposes only. Consult your oncologist for medical advice.

## Types of Oncology Infusion Support

### Immunotherapy Infusions
Immunotherapy drugs are among the most important advances in cancer treatment:
- Checkpoint inhibitors allow the immune system to attack cancer
- Monoclonal antibodies target specific cancer proteins
- Immunotherapy can provide lasting anti-cancer effects

### Iron Infusions
Cancer and its treatment often cause anemia. IV iron can:
- Rapidly correct iron deficiency
- Improve energy and quality of life
- Support patients during chemotherapy
- Prepare patients for surgery

### Supportive Infusions
Other infusion therapies may be needed during cancer treatment:
- Hydration therapy
- Anti-nausea medications
- Other supportive medications
