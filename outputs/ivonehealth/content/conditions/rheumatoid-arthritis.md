## About Rheumatoid Arthritis

Rheumatoid arthritis (RA) is a chronic autoimmune condition that primarily affects the joints, causing inflammation, pain, swelling, and potential joint damage. Early and effective treatment is important to prevent disease progression.

## How Infusion Therapy Helps RA

Infusion therapies offer targeted treatment options for rheumatoid arthritis. These treatments work by:

- Suppressing the overactive immune response
- Reducing joint inflammation and pain
- Preventing progressive joint damage
- Improving quality of life and function
