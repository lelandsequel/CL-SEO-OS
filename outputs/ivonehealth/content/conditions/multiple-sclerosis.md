## About Multiple Sclerosis

Multiple sclerosis (MS) is a chronic autoimmune condition where the immune system attacks the protective covering of nerve fibers in the central nervous system. This can cause a range of symptoms including fatigue, mobility issues, and cognitive changes.

## How Infusion Therapy Helps MS

Infusion therapies have become an important option for managing MS. These treatments work by:

- Modulating the immune response to reduce disease activity
- Decreasing inflammation in the central nervous system
- Slowing disease progression
- Reducing the frequency and severity of relapses
