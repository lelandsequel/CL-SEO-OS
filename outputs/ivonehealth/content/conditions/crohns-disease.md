## About Crohn's Disease

Crohn's disease is a type of inflammatory bowel disease (IBD) that causes chronic inflammation of the digestive tract. It can affect any part of the GI tract and lead to symptoms including abdominal pain, diarrhea, fatigue, and malnutrition.

## How Infusion Therapy Helps Crohn's Disease

Biologic infusion therapies have transformed Crohn's disease management. These treatments work by:

- Targeting specific inflammatory proteins like TNF-alpha
- Reducing intestinal inflammation
- Promoting mucosal healing
- Achieving and maintaining remission
