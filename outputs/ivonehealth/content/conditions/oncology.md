## About Oncology Infusion Support

Cancer patients often require specialized infusion services as part of their treatment plan. These infusions may include immunotherapy to fight cancer and supportive therapies to manage treatment side effects.

## How Infusion Therapy Supports Oncology Patients

Infusion therapies play multiple roles in oncology care:

- Immunotherapy activates the immune system to target cancer cells
- Iron infusions address anemia caused by cancer or its treatment
- Supportive infusions help manage symptoms and side effects
- Maintenance infusions continue care between oncology appointments

## Available Infusion Therapies

IV One Health provides infusion services for oncology patients:

- [Immunotherapy](/services/immunotherapy) — Checkpoint inhibitors, monoclonal antibodies, and other immune-based treatments
- [Iron Infusion](/services/iron-infusion) — Treatment for cancer-related iron deficiency anemia
